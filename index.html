ABOUT US

Welcome to Maharaja Cricket Club, where passion meets play! Nestled in the vibrant city of Jaipur, our club is dedicated to providing an exceptional cricket experience for enthusiasts of all ages and skill levels.

Cricket Lessons

Club provides personalized coaching, skill development in batting, bowling, and fielding, fitness training, game strategies, and regular practice sessions to elevate your cricket performance.

Test Cricket

Club provides test cricket lessons focusing on technique, mental toughness, stamina, and strategic adaptability for success in long-format matches.

T20 Cricket

Club provides T20 lessons focusing on aggressive batting, quick decision-making, sharp fielding, and effective bowling strategies for fast-paced matches.
